Volunteer & Internship Opportunities

Volunteers are the heart of our organization and allow us to offer the many services at Street Level.

Volunteers help us in a variety of capacities and range in experience to include day laborers, students, community members, and healthcare providers.

We especially seek volunteers with fluency in one or more of the following languages: Spanish, Mam, Mongolian, Nepali, and Tigrinya.

Street Level Health Project accepts medical providers with MDs, NPs, and PAs with a valid California license. Residents are welcome as long as they are fully licensed. Volunteer providers typically have training in internal medicine, family medicine, or emergency medicine.

We currently have specific needs at Street Level Health Project, particularly on our Immigrant Rights and Empowerment and Health Navigation teams. Please see the list of volunteer descriptions below.

  • Occupational Health & Safety Outreach Internship
  • Know Your Rights Outreach Internship
  • Oakland Worker’s Collective Intake Internship
  • Community Health Navigator
  • Consultas Naturistas Volunteer Program Assistant

 

If you are interested in any of the three positions with Immigrant Rights and Empowerment, please send an email with your name, resume (if you have one) and description of your interest to Edgar Salazar at edgar@streetlevelhealthproject.org.

If you are interested in being a Community Health Navigator, please send an email with your name, resume (if you have one), your level of Spanish fluency, the hours you could dedicate and description of your interest to Juan Cano at juan@streetlevelhealthproject.org.

If you are interested in being a Consultas Naturistas Volunteer Program Assistant please send an email with your name, resume (if you have one), your level of Spanish fluency, the hours you could dedicate and description of your interest to Karla Prodigue at karla@streetlevelhealthproject.org.

We always have a specific need at Street Level for volunteer clinical providers in the clinic. If you are a MDs, NPs, and PAs with a valid California license, please complete the form below.

Although we do not currently have other volunteer needs at the organization, if you would like to complete the general form below so we have your information on file, we will be in touch as our needs change.

Thank you!
